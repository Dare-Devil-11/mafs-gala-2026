About the Gala

Serving our community
for 34 years.

The MAFS Annual Gala is our flagship fundraising event — bringing together community leaders, families, and advocates for an unforgettable evening of fine dining, entertainment, and meaningful impact.

Every dollar raised supports Metropolitan Asian Family Services programs serving immigrant families, seniors, and youth across Chicagoland — providing case management, literacy services, and crisis support.

Evening Program

The Evening's Agenda

5:30 PM
VVIP Private Reception
An exclusive pre-gala gathering for VVIP guests — cocktails, canapés, and private access to MAFS leadership.
6:00 PM
Doors Open & Cocktail Hour
Welcome reception with cocktails, hors d'oeuvres, and networking. Live ambient music sets the evening's tone.
7:00 PM
Welcome & Dinner Service
Opening remarks from MAFS leadership. Three-course dinner begins. Cultural entertainment performance.
8:00 PM
Awards & Recognition
Honoring community champions, outstanding volunteers, and partner organizations driving impact across Chicagoland.
8:45 PM
Keynote & Fund-a-Dream
Inspiring keynote followed by our live fund-a-dream fundraising appeal. Every gift directly funds family services.
9:30 PM
Dancing & Celebration
Live DJ, dancing, dessert, and continued networking. An unforgettable close to a spectacular evening.
